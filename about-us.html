allyance is a social enterprise not for profit in-school counselling service.

The aim of the allyance in-school counselling service is to provide counselling support when directed by the headteachers to:

  • Improve the emotional wellbeing of children aged 4 to 11 years, in order to improve their learning potential.
  • To work with school staff, parents and carers to help everyone understand the childâ€™s feelings and behaviour.
  • To respond to long and short term needs of children and their families in schools.

    • We provide counselling to children with a range of mental health issues including:

  • Family breakdown.
  • Friendship issues.
  • Bereavement.
  • Victims of domestic violence.
  • Physical and mental abuse.
  • Low self-esteem.
  • Self-harming.
  • Social difficulties.

    • We believe that strong mental health enables young people to:

  • Contribute to their community.
  • Feel inspired.
  • Form good relationships.
  • Have a desire for learning.
  • Become resilient employees/employers of the future.
